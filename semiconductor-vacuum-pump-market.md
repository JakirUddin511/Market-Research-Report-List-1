<p><p>The semiconductor vacuum pump market features several key types essential for creating and maintaining low-pressure environments in manufacturing processes. Dry pumps eliminate oil contamination, making them suitable for sensitive applications. Multi-stage roots pumps offer high pumping speeds and efficiency for large volumes. Turbomolecular pumps achieve ultra-high vacuum levels, crucial for precision semiconductor fabrication. Claw pumps provide a compact and efficient solution, combining features of both dry and roots pumps while ensuring reliability in various operational conditions.</p></p>
