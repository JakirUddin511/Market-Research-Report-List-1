<p><p>Terahertz sensing technology encompasses two primary market types: photonic sensing and electronic sensing. Photonic sensing leverages terahertz waves generated and manipulated using photonic devices, enabling high-resolution imaging and spectroscopy for applications in material characterization and quality control. In contrast, electronic sensing utilizes electronic components to detect terahertz frequencies, offering advantages in compactness and integration with existing electronic systems. Both types serve diverse fields, including biomedical diagnostics, security screening, and industrial automation, driving innovation and advancements in sensing applications.</p></p>
