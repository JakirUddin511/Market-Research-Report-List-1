<p><p>Takaful operates within two primary market types: Life/Family Takaful and General Takaful. Life/Family Takaful focuses on providing financial protection and savings plans for families, encompassing life insurance benefits, investment opportunities, and risk coverage in the event of death or disability. In contrast, General Takaful covers non-life risks, including property, liability, and health, facilitating protection against potential losses. Both markets adhere to Sharia principles, promoting mutual cooperation and shared responsibility among participants while ensuring ethical investment practices.</p></p>
