<p><p>The speed reducer market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Gear Reducers** use gear sets to reduce speed and increase torque, commonly used in industrial applications.</p><p>2. **Worm Wheel Reducers** utilize a worm gear mechanism, offering high reduction ratios and compact design.</p><p>3. **Planetary Gears** feature multiple gears rotating around a central gear, providing high efficiency and space savings.</p><p>4. **Cycloid Reducers** employ an eccentric rotation mechanism, offering advantages in torque and size.</p><p>5. **Harmonic Drives** utilize flexible gears for smooth motion and precision, ideal for robotics and aerospace.</p></p>
