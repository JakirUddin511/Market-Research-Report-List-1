<p><p>The Emergency Ceiling Light market is categorized into three main types based on power systems. Self-Contained Power Systems integrate their own battery and charging mechanisms, providing direct illumination during outages. Central Power Systems rely on a centralized energy source to power multiple lights, ensuring synchronized operation across large areas. Hybrid Power Systems combine both approaches, utilizing central power when available while having the flexibility of self-contained batteries for backup, thereby enhancing reliability in emergency lighting situations.</p></p>
