<p><p>The process gas screw compressors market is divided into two main types: oil-free and oil-injected screw compressors. Oil-free screw compressors operate without lubrication oil, making them ideal for industries requiring clean, uncontaminated gas, such as food and pharmaceuticals. In contrast, oil-injected screw compressors use oil for lubrication and cooling, allowing for higher efficiency and performance in various applications, including petrochemical and power generation. Both types cater to specific operational needs and environmental requirements across diverse sectors.</p></p>
