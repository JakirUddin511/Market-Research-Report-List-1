<p><strong>The Glass Block Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tempered Glass Block</li><li>Annealed Glass Block</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Block market encompasses two primary types: tempered glass blocks and annealed glass blocks. Tempered glass blocks are heat-strengthened, providing enhanced durability and resistance to thermal stress, making them ideal for safety and high-impact applications. In contrast, annealed glass blocks undergo a slower cooling process, resulting in a more uniform internal structure but with lesser strength compared to tempered variants. Both types serve various architectural and design purposes, offering aesthetic appeal and functional benefits in construction and decorative applications.</p></p>
