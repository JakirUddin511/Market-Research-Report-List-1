<p><p>Single trip travel insurance encompasses two main market types: personal insurance and group insurance. Personal insurance targets individual travelers, providing coverage for unforeseen events like trip cancellations, medical emergencies, or lost luggage during a single journey. In contrast, group insurance caters to a collective, such as families or organizations, offering a bundled coverage option at a reduced rate. Both types ensure travelers have financial protection and peace of mind, though they differ in scope and pricing structure based on the number of insured individuals.</p></p>
