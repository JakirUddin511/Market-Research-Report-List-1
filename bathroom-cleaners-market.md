<p><p>The bathroom cleaners market features various types, with alkali and strong acid cleaners being prominent. Alkali cleaners, often containing ingredients like sodium hydroxide, effectively break down soap scum and grease, making them suitable for regular maintenance. In contrast, strong acid cleaners, such as those with hydrochloric or phosphoric acid, are designed for heavy-duty tasks, efficiently removing mineral deposits and rust stains. Both types serve distinct purposes, catering to different cleaning needs and preferences in maintaining bathroom hygiene.</p></p>
