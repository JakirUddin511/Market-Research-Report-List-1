<p><p>Masonry cement is classified into several types based on strength and application. Type N is a general-purpose cement suitable for non-load-bearing masonry. Type M offers higher strength, ideal for heavy structural applications. Type S is specifically designed for high lateral strength, commonly used in seismically active areas. Other types may include specialized formulations for particular environmental conditions, such as sulfate resistance or improved workability, catering to diverse construction needs. Each type is essential for ensuring durability and performance in masonry work.</p></p>
