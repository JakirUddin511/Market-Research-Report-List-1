<p><ul><li>Structural Oil Pan</li><li>Non-Structural Oil Pan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive oil pan market is segmented into two main types: structural and non-structural oil pans. Structural oil pans are integral to the engine's overall structure, providing strength and supporting engine components while also containing the oil. Non-structural oil pans, on the other hand, primarily serve as reservoirs for engine oil without contributing to the engine's structural integrity. Both types are essential for engine lubrication and efficiency, catering to various vehicle designs and performance requirements.</p></p>
