<p><p>The Optical Network Component and Subsystem market is categorized into three main types: Active Components, Passive Components, and Optical Modules. Active Components, such as optical amplifiers and transceivers, require power to function and play a crucial role in signal processing and transmission. Passive Components, like filters and splitters, do not require external power and facilitate the routing and management of optical signals. Optical Modules, encompassing integrated technologies, combine functions for seamless connectivity, enhancing the performance and efficiency of optical networks.</p></p>
