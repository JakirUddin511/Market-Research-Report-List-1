<p><p>The Smart Automotive Key Market primarily consists of two types: Passive Keyless Access (PKA) and Remote Keyless Access (RKA). Passive Keyless Access allows users to unlock and start their vehicles without physically pressing buttons, utilizing proximity sensors to detect the key fob nearby. In contrast, Remote Keyless Access enables users to lock, unlock, or start their vehicles from a distance using a remote control. Both technologies enhance convenience and security, reflecting the industry's shift towards advanced automotive access solutions.</p></p>
