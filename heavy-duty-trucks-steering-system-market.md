<p><p>The heavy-duty truck steering system market features two primary types: Electric Power Steering (EPS) and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS). EPS utilizes electric motors to provide steering assistance, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing maintenance needs. In contrast, EHPS combines hydraulic systems with electric controls, offering a blend of traditional hydraulic benefits and advanced electronic responsiveness. Both systems aim to improve maneuverability, reduce driver effort, and enhance safety, catering to the evolving needs of the heavy-duty trucking industry.</p></p>
