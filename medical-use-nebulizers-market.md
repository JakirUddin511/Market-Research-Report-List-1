<p><p>The medical use nebulizers market is categorized into three main types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and mesh nebulizers. Pneumatic nebulizers, driven by compressed air, convert liquid medication into aerosol for inhalation. Ultrasonic nebulizers utilize high-frequency vibrations to aerosolize solutions, offering quieter operation and faster delivery. Mesh nebulizers use a vibrating mesh to create fine aerosol droplets, providing efficient medication delivery with less waste. Each type plays a crucial role in respiratory therapy, catering to varying patient needs and treatment requirements.</p></p>
