<p><p>The epoxy resin repair mortars market is categorized into solvent-based and solvent-free types. Solvent-based epoxy mortars contain organic solvents that enhance application ease and performance but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, solvent-free epoxy mortars lack these solvents, offering lower VOC emissions and improved environmental compliance, making them ideal for indoor applications. Both types are widely used in construction, industrial maintenance, and infrastructure repair due to their durability and adhesion properties, catering to varying project requirements.</p></p>
