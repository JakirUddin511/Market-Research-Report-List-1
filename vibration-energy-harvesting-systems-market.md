<p><p>Vibration energy harvesting systems capture energy from vibrations to power electronic devices. The market is categorized into linear, nonlinear, and rotational systems. Linear systems convert vibrational energy through direct displacement methods, offering simplicity and efficiency. Nonlinear systems harness energy from complex vibration patterns, providing greater adaptability and often enhanced output in variable conditions. Rotational systems focus on converting energy from rotational motion, catering to applications where vibrations are less dominant but rotational dynamics are significant, expanding energy harvesting possibilities.</p></p>
