<p><p>Double-walled water baths are specialized equipment used primarily in laboratories for temperature control. They come in two main types: rectangular and round. Rectangular water baths typically provide more surface area, making them ideal for larger containers and multiple samples. Conversely, round water baths offer uniform heating and are often better for smaller samples due to their design, which minimizes temperature gradients. Both types ensure precise temperature regulation, crucial for experiments requiring stable thermal conditions.</p></p>
