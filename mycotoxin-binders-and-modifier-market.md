<p><p>Mycotoxin binders and modifiers are substances used in the feed and food industry to mitigate the adverse effects of mycotoxins—toxins produced by fungi that can contaminate crops. The rising awareness of food safety, coupled with stringent regulatory frameworks, has driven the demand for mycotoxin management solutions.</p><p>The Mycotoxin Binders and Modifier Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period.
