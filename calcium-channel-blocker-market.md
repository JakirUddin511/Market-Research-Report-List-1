<p><p>Calcium Channel Blockers (CCBs) are a class of medications that inhibit the influx of calcium ions through voltage-gated calcium channels in cardiac and vascular smooth muscle. This action leads to decreased myocardial contractility and relaxation of vascular smooth muscle, resulting in lowered blood pressure, reduced cardiac workload, and improved oxygen supply to the heart. CCBs are commonly used to treat conditions such as hypertension, angina, and certain arrhythmias.</p><p>The Calcium Channel Blocker Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.00% during the forecast period. Key drivers of market growth include the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, increasing aging population, and growing awareness around hypertension management. Moreover, the trend towards personalized medicine and the integration of digital health technologies in treatment regimens are shaping the future landscape of the market.
