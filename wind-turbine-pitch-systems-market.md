<p><p>Wind turbine pitch systems are essential for optimizing rotor blade angles to maximize energy production and protect turbines from damage during high winds. The hydraulic pitch system uses fluid pressure to adjust blade angles, offering rapid response and reliability, often preferred in larger turbines. In contrast, the electrical pitch system employs electric motors and sensors for precise control and simplicity, making it suitable for smaller or modern turbine designs. Both systems play crucial roles in enhancing turbine efficiency and safety.</p></p>
