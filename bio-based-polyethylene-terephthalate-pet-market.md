<p><p>Bio-based Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is produced through three primary methods: from biomass, from bio-based precursors, and from bacteria. Biomass-derived PET is sourced from organic materials such as crops. Bio-based precursors involve using renewable substances to create chemical building blocks for PET synthesis. Bacteria-based production utilizes microorganisms to convert renewable resources directly into PET, offering a sustainable alternative to traditional petroleum-based methods. Each approach aims to reduce environmental impact and reliance on fossil fuels while maintaining material performance.</p></p>
