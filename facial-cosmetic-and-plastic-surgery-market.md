<p><p>The facial cosmetic and plastic surgery market can be categorized into two main types: implants and injectables. Implants involve the surgical insertion of materials, such as silicone or other biomaterials, to enhance features like cheeks, chin, or lips, offering permanent results. In contrast, injectables include non-surgical treatments such as dermal fillers and botulinum toxin, providing temporary solutions to wrinkles and volume loss. Both segments cater to aesthetic improvement but differ in permanence, procedure invasiveness, and recovery time.</p></p>
