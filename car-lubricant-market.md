<p><strong>The Car Lubricant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Engine Oil</li><li>Gear Oil</li><li>Transmission Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car lubricant market includes engine oil, gear oil, and transmission fluid, each serving distinct purposes. Engine oil reduces friction, cools, and cleans internal combustion engines. Gear oil protects gears and bearings in differentials and manual transmissions, ensuring smooth operation under high pressure. Transmission fluid lubricates, cools, and facilitates gear shifts in automatic and manual transmissions, enhancing performance and longevity. All three lubricants are essential for vehicle efficiency, durability, and optimal functioning, tailored to specific mechanical systems.</p></p>
