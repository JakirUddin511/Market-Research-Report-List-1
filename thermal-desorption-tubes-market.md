<p><p>The thermal desorption tubes market encompasses two main types: single-bed and multiple-bed thermal desorption tubes. Single-bed thermal desorption tubes contain one section for capturing and desorbing volatile organic compounds (VOCs), ideal for simpler applications requiring minimal sample complexity. In contrast, multiple-bed thermal desorption tubes incorporate multiple sorbent sections, enabling the collection of various compounds simultaneously. This design is beneficial for complex environmental samples, providing enhanced sensitivity and specificity for diverse analytes in air, water, and soil monitoring.</p></p>
