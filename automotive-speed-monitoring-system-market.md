<p><p>Automotive Speed Monitoring Systems encompass various technologies to track and manage vehicle speeds. Microwave RADAR-based systems utilize high-frequency signals to detect speed, while laser-based systems employ focused light beams for precise measurements. Ultrasonic-based systems use sound waves for speed detection. Camera-based monitoring captures visual data to monitor speed and traffic violations, while intrusive sensors and road tubes physically measure vehicle speeds on the road. Satellite-based road traffic monitoring leverages satellite technology for widespread coverage, and other systems include diverse, innovative approaches to speed monitoring.</p></p>
