<p><p>The carbon fiber epoxy market is segmented into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based systems typically offer superior adhesion, durability, and resistance to environmental factors, making them ideal for high-performance applications. However, they may release volatile organic compounds (VOCs), raising environmental concerns. In contrast, water-based systems are more environmentally friendly, emitting fewer VOCs, and are easier to clean. While they might have slightly lower performance in some applications, their safety and sustainability appeal to various industries seeking greener alternatives.</p></p>
