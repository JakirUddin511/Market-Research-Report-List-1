<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Embedded Buzzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Electromagnetic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded buzzer market is primarily classified into two types: piezoelectric and electromagnetic. Piezoelectric buzzers use materials that generate sound through the piezoelectric effect, often preferred for their compact size, low power consumption, and durability, making them popular in consumer electronics and alarms. Electromagnetic buzzers, on the other hand, operate using a magnetic coil and diaphragm, producing sound through electromagnetic induction. They are typically larger and louder, suitable for applications requiring distinct acoustic signals, such as automotive or industrial settings.</p></p>
