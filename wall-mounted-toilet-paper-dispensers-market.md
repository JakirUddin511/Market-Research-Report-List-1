<p><p>Wall-mounted toilet paper dispensers are categorized into two main types: manual and automatic. Manual dispensers require users to pull the paper by hand, offering a cost-effective solution with straightforward maintenance. They are often designed for high-traffic areas where durability is essential. In contrast, automatic dispensers utilize sensors to dispense paper when hands are detected, promoting hygiene and reducing waste. These modern options are increasingly favored in facilities prioritizing cleanliness and user convenience, reflecting evolving consumer expectations.</p></p>
