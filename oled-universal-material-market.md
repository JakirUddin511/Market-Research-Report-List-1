<p><p>The OLED Universal Material Market consists of several key types: ETL (Electron Transport Layer), EIL (Electron Injection Layer), HIL (Hole Injection Layer), and HTL (Hole Transport Layer). ETL enhances electron movement towards emitting layers, while EIL facilitates efficient electron injection. HIL is crucial for injecting holes, and HTL enables the movement of these holes to the emitting layer. Other markets include various ancillary materials and processes that support the performance and longevity of OLED displays in various applications.</p></p>
