<p><p>The Electric Vehicle (EV) Tachograph market includes two main types: Integrated and Portable. Integrated tachographs are built directly into the vehicle's system, offering seamless connectivity and data synchronization with the EV's telematics. They are ideal for fleet management and compliance tracking. Portable tachographs, on the other hand, are standalone devices that can be moved between vehicles, providing flexibility for smaller fleets or temporary use. Both types ensure accurate recording of driving data, essential for regulatory compliance and operational efficiency in EV fleets.</p></p>
