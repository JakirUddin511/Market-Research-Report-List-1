<p><p>Teeth whitening gels primarily utilize two key types of whitening agents: carbamide peroxide and hydrogen peroxide. Carbamide peroxide, a compound that breaks down into hydrogen peroxide and urea, provides a slower release of whitening effects, making it suitable for at-home use. Hydrogen peroxide, a stronger oxidizing agent, delivers quicker results and is often found in professional treatments. Both types are effective for removing stains, enhancing dental aesthetics, and boosting consumer demand in the teeth whitening market.</p></p>
