<p><p>The autoinjector market is divided into two main types: fillable autoinjectors and prefilled autoinjectors. Fillable autoinjectors allow users to manually fill the device with a medication before use, offering flexibility in dosage and medication choice. In contrast, prefilled autoinjectors come preloaded with the medication, providing convenience and reducing preparation time. Both types are designed for self-administration, enhancing patient compliance and enabling effective management of chronic conditions by facilitating easy access to necessary treatments.</p></p>
