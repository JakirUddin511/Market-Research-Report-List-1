<p><p>The automotive airbag fabric market features distinct types, including Flat Airbag Fabric and One-Piece Woven (OPW) Airbag Fabric. Flat Airbag Fabric is characterized by its traditional construction, providing essential strength and durability for airbags. Conversely, OPW Airbag Fabric utilizes a seamless weaving technique, resulting in increased integrity and reduced vulnerability to leakage. Both types are crucial for ensuring passenger safety during collisions, with OPW gaining popularity for its enhanced performance characteristics and efficiency in manufacturing.</p></p>
