<p><p>The Linear DC Servomotor market is categorized into three main voltage types: low voltage, mid voltage, and high voltage. Low voltage servomotors, typically operating under 48 volts, are favored for compact applications with limited power requirements. Mid voltage motors, ranging from 48 to 400 volts, offer enhanced performance for moderate load applications. High voltage servomotors, exceeding 400 volts, are designed for heavy-duty industrial uses, providing higher efficiency and greater power output for demanding operations such as automation and robotics.</p></p>
