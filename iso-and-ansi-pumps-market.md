<p><p>The ISO and ANSI pumps market is divided into two main types: magdrive pumps and sealed pumps. Magdrive pumps feature a magnetic drive system that eliminates shaft seals, reducing leakage risks and maintenance needs, making them ideal for handling hazardous fluids. In contrast, sealed pumps rely on traditional mechanical seals to maintain fluid containment, offering a robust solution for various applications. Both types cater to industries requiring reliable fluid transfer while adhering to international standards for performance and safety.</p></p>
