<p><p>The insulin pumps and accessories market is categorized into three main types: tethered insulin pumps, patch insulin pumps, and consumables. Tethered insulin pumps are traditional devices connected to the body via tubing, allowing continuous insulin delivery. Patch insulin pumps are wireless, adhesive devices worn on the skin that provide a more discreet option for insulin delivery. The consumables market includes necessary supplies like infusion sets, reservoirs, and other accessories essential for the effective operation of both types of pumps.</p></p>
