<p><p>Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) systems encompass various technologies for seamless toll transactions. Vehicle Automatic Understanding Systems utilize onboard equipment to automatically identify vehicles, enabling effortless toll deductions. Short Range Communication (SRC) relies on proximity-based systems, where vehicles communicate with roadside units for instant toll processing. Global Positioning Satellite (GPS) systems provide location tracking to charge tolls based on distance traveled. These technologies enhance efficiency and reduce traffic congestion while offering a convenient way for drivers to pay tolls electronically.</p></p>
