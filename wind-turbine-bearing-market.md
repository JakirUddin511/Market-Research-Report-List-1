<p><p>The wind turbine bearing market includes various types of bearings crucial for optimal turbine performance. Spherical roller bearings, known for their ability to accommodate misalignment and heavy loads, are commonly used in the gearbox and generator. Both types enhance reliability and efficiency, contributing to the overall longevity and performance of wind turbines in a rapidly growing renewable energy sector.</p></p>
