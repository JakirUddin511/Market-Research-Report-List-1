<p><p>Discrete semiconductor modules are key components in power electronics, categorized into several types. Standard (Non-Integrated) IGBT Modules offer robust switching capabilities without integrated control. Intelligent Power Modules combine IGBTs with drive components for enhanced efficiency. Thyristor/Diode Modules facilitate controlled rectification and switching. Power Integrated Modules integrate power devices for compact designs, improving thermal performance. MOSFET Modules are used for fast switching applications due to their low on-resistance and high efficiency, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
