<p><p>Flooded lead-acid batteries are commonly used in vehicles and come in two main types: General Flooded Lead-Acid Batteries and Enhanced Flooded Batteries (EFB). General Flooded Lead-Acid Batteries are traditional, cost-effective options suitable for standard vehicles. In contrast, EFBs offer improved performance, enhanced cycle life, and increased resistance to deep discharges, making them ideal for stop-start applications and vehicles with high electrical demands. Both types provide reliable energy storage but cater to different needs based on vehicle requirements.</p></p>
