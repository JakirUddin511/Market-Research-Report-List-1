<p><p>The automotive pneumatic equipment market is categorized into two main types: compressed air and compressed inert gases. Compressed air systems utilize atmospheric air to power various tools and machinery in automotive applications, offering versatility and convenience. On the other hand, compressed inert gases, such as nitrogen or argon, are used for specialized applications requiring a non-reactive environment, providing benefits like reduced oxidation and improved performance. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing efficiency and safety in automotive operations.</p></p>
