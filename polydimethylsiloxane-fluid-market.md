<p><p>Polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS) fluid is categorized based on viscosity into low, medium, and high viscosity types, each serving distinct applications. Low viscosity PDMS is fluid and has excellent flow characteristics, making it ideal for lubrication and coating. Medium viscosity PDMS strikes a balance, suitable for adhesives and sealants. High viscosity PDMS is thicker, providing enhanced stability and resistance, often used in construction, automotive, and medical applications. Each type's unique properties cater to various industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
