<p><p>The offshore oil and gas drilling market comprises various platform types, including jackups, semi-submersibles, and drillships. Jackups are mobile platforms with long legs that can be elevated above water, ideal for shallow waters. Semi-submersibles are anchored vessels that float on the water surface, suitable for deeper locations due to their stability. Drillships are specially designed ships equipped for drilling, capable of operating in ultra-deep waters. These platforms cater to different depth requirements and environmental conditions in offshore exploration.</p></p>
