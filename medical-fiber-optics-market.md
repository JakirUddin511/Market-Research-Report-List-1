<p><ul><li>Silica Fiber/Glass Fiber Optics</li><li>Plastic Fiber Optics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical fiber optics market is primarily divided into two types: silica fiber optics and plastic fiber optics. Silica fiber optics, made from glass, offer high precision, durability, and superior light transmission, making them ideal for applications like endoscopy and laser surgeries. In contrast, plastic fiber optics are more flexible, lightweight, and cost-effective, suitable for less demanding medical applications such as illumination and simple imaging. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing medical procedures and diagnostics.</p></p>
