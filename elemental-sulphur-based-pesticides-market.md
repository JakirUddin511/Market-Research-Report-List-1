<p><p>Elemental sulfur-based pesticides are categorized into two primary market types: dispersion and powder formulations. They are effective in various environmental conditions. Powder formulations, on the other hand, are dry and can be easily spread on crops, allowing for targeted treatment and aiding in the control of pests and diseases. Both types are essential for sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
