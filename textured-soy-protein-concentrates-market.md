<p><p>Textured Soy Protein Concentrates (TSPC) are primarily available in two market types: granules and flour. Granules are coarser and often used in meat analogs, ready-to-eat meals, and snacks, providing a chewy texture. Flour, on the other hand, is finely ground and frequently utilized in baking, protein supplements, and as a binding agent in various food products. Both forms serve as versatile sources of plant-based protein, catering to the growing demand for vegetarian and vegan food options.</p></p>
