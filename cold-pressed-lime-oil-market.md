<p><p>The Cold Pressed Lime Oil market is primarily categorized into two types: Mexican lime (Citrus aurantiifolia) and Tahitian lime (Citrus latifolia). Mexican lime is known for its intense aroma and higher acidity, making it popular in culinary applications and beverages. In contrast, Tahitian lime has a milder flavor and larger fruit size, often favored for its juiciness and versatility in cooking and cocktails. Both types are valued for their essential oils in aromatherapy, cosmetics, and cleaning products.</p></p>
