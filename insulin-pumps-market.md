<p><strong>The Insulin Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Pump</li><li>Patch Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulin pump market comprises various types, including traditional insulin pumps and patch pumps. Traditional pumps consist of a reservoir, infusion set, and a control unit, allowing users to deliver precise insulin doses. They are typically worn on a belt or in a pocket. In contrast, patch pumps are small, adhesive devices affixed directly to the skin, delivering insulin through a cannula without external tubing. Both options provide users with improved blood glucose management and greater flexibility in their diabetes care.</p></p>
