<p><p>The inspection wells market comprises various types, including rotational molding inspection wells and welded piping inspection wells. Rotational molding inspection wells are created using a molding process that ensures uniform thickness and durability, making them ideal for diverse environments. In contrast, welded piping inspection wells involve the assembly of pipes through welding, ensuring robust connections and enhanced leak resistance. Both types serve critical functions in monitoring groundwater and facilitating access for maintenance in various industrial and environmental applications.</p></p>
