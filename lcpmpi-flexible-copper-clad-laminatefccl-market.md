<p><p>LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) and MPI (Metal-Polyimide) Flexible Copper Clad Laminates (FCCLs) are advanced materials used in flexible printed circuit boards. LCP FCCL offers excellent thermal stability, high-frequency performance, and low moisture absorption, making it suitable for high-speed applications. Conversely, MPI FCCL combines metal layers with polyimide substrates, providing enhanced mechanical properties and flexibility for diverse electronics. Both types cater to industries like telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics, driving innovation in compact and efficient electronic designs.</p></p>
