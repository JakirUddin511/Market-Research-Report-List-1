<p><strong>The Optical Patterned Wafer Inspection Equipment (OPWIE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bright-Field Inspection Systems</li><li>Dark-Field Inspection Systems</li><li>Electron Beam Inspection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Patterned Wafer Inspection Equipment (OPWIE) is crucial for semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring the quality of wafers by detecting defects. Bright-field inspection systems utilize transmitted light to highlight features, making defects visible against a bright background. Dark-field inspection systems leverage oblique illumination to reveal defects against a dark background, enhancing sensitivity for small imperfections. Electron beam inspection systems employ focused beams of electrons for high-resolution defect detection, offering superior precision for advanced technology nodes. Together, these systems enhance quality control in semiconductor production.</p></p>
