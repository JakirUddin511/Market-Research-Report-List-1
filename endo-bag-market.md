<p><p>An endo bag, or endoscopic bag, is a medical device used during minimally invasive surgeries to retrieve excised tissue or organs through small incisions. It is designed to facilitate the collection and removal of specimens without the need for larger incisions, thus minimizing trauma and promoting quicker recovery for patients. The endo bag market is experiencing notable growth, driven by advancements in surgical techniques and the increasing prevalence of minimally invasive procedures. </p><p>The Endo Bag Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.40% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Endo Bag market is categorized into two main types: detachable and non-detachable. Detachable Endo Bags allow for easy separation from the retrieval device after use, facilitating safe and efficient specimen handling. In contrast, non-detachable Endo Bags remain attached throughout the procedure, ensuring the contents are secure until retrieval. Each type serves specific surgical needs, influencing surgeon preference based on procedural requirements, ease of use, and specimen management during minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
