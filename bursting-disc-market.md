<p><p>The bursting disc market features three primary types: Positive Arch, Anti Arch, and Flat Type bursting discs. Positive Arch bursting discs have a concave design that allows for optimal pressure relief, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Anti Arch discs, with a convex shape, provide increased stability under varying pressures, reducing the risk of premature failure. Flat Type discs are versatile and commonly used in various industries due to their ease of installation and reliability. Each type serves specific safety and operational needs.</p></p>
