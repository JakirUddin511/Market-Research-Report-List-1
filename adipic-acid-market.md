<p><p>The Adipic Acid market can be segmented into three main types based on production methods: Cyclohexane oxidation, Cyclohexene oxidation, and Phenol hydrogenation. The Cyclohexane oxidation method involves the oxidation of cyclohexane with nitric acid to produce adipic acid, which is widely used in nylon production. Cyclohexene oxidation follows a similar process but uses cyclohexene as the starting material, offering efficiency advantages. Phenol hydrogenation involves reducing phenol to produce adipic acid, providing an alternative pathway that may utilize different feedstocks.</p></p>
