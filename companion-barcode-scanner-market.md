<p><p>The Companion Barcode Scanner market can be categorized into two primary types: 1D and 2D scanners. 1D scanners read traditional linear barcodes, typically seen on retail products and shipping labels. They capture data in a single dimension, making them efficient for straightforward scanning tasks. Conversely, 2D scanners can read matrix barcodes, such as QR codes, which store more information in two dimensions. This capability allows for versatile applications, including mobile payments and inventory management, making them essential in various industries.</p></p>
