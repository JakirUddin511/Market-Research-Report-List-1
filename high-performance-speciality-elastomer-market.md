<p><strong>The High Performance Speciality Elastomer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE)</li><li>Thermosetting Elastomer (TSE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Performance Specialty Elastomer market is primarily divided into two types: Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) and Thermosetting Elastomers (TSE). TPEs are versatile materials that combine the properties of rubber and plastics, offering flexibility, durability, and recyclability, making them ideal for automotive and consumer goods applications. Conversely, TSEs are characterized by their irreversible curing process, resulting in superior heat resistance, chemical stability, and mechanical properties, making them suitable for high-temperature and high-stress applications in industries like aerospace and electronics.</p></p>
