<p><ul><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li><li>Hall Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wheel Speed Sensor ABS market consists of two main types: Magnetic Electric Type and Hall Effect Type. Magnetic Electric sensors rely on electromagnetic induction to generate a voltage signal proportional to wheel speed. They are robust and effective in harsh conditions. Hall Effect sensors, on the other hand, use magnetic fields to produce a digital signal, offering high accuracy and responsiveness. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety by facilitating anti-lock braking systems (ABS) through real-time speed monitoring.</p></p>
