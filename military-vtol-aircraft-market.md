<p><p>The Military VTOL (Vertical Takeoff and Landing) Aircraft Market includes **Powered-lift VTOL Aircraft** and **Rotorcraft**. Powered-lift VTOL aircraft, like tiltrotors, combine fixed-wing and rotor capabilities for high-speed, long-range missions. Rotorcraft, such as helicopters, rely solely on rotating blades for vertical lift and maneuverability, excelling in close-range operations and versatility. Both types are critical for military applications, offering unique advantages in logistics, reconnaissance, and combat, with powered-lift aircraft focusing on speed and range, while rotorcraft prioritize agility and adaptability in diverse terrains.</p></p>
