<p><p>MIM (Metal Injection Molding) binders are essential for shaping metal powders into complex parts. The paraffin-based binder provides a low-cost, versatile option, known for its excellent flow characteristics and ease of removal during debinding. In contrast, oil-based binders, often featuring higher viscosity, offer enhanced mechanical properties and improved surface finish. Both types cater to different application requirements, impacting factors such as production efficiency, part quality, and the intricacy of the designs produced in the MIM process.</p></p>
