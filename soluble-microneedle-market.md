<p><p>The soluble microneedle market comprises two key types: Quadrangular Pyramids Type and Cones Type. In contrast, Cones Type microneedles have a conical shape, designed for targeted application and stability during insertion. Both types facilitate pain-free delivery of medications or vaccines, improving patient compliance and offering innovative solutions in transdermal therapies. Their distinct designs cater to various therapeutic needs and skin types.</p></p>
