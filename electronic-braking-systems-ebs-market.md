<p><p>Electronic Braking Systems (EBS) enhance vehicle safety and performance by providing precise brake control through electronic regulation. The market is primarily divided into two types: Disc EBS and Drum EBS. Disc EBS utilizes brake discs for improved heat dissipation and responsiveness, making it ideal for high-performance vehicles. In contrast, Drum EBS uses brake drums, typically found in heavier vehicles for better force application and stability. Both systems play a crucial role in modern automotive braking technology, adapting to various vehicle needs.</p></p>
