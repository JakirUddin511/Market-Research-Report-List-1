<p><strong>The Industrial Cartridge Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw-in Cartridge Valve</li><li>Slip-in Cartridge Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial cartridge valves market comprises two main types: screw-in and slip-in cartridge valves. </p><p>Screw-in cartridge valves feature a threaded design that allows for easy installation and replacement, making them popular for space-constrained applications. In contrast, slip-in cartridge valves utilize a slip-fit design that simplifies integration into hydraulic systems, offering flexibility and ease of maintenance. Both types are essential components in hydraulic systems, providing control over fluid flow, pressure, and direction, thus ensuring efficient operation in various industrial applications.</p></p>
