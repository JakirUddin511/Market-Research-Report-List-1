<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Phytoremediation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phytosequestration</li><li>Rhizodegradation</li><li>Phytohydraulics</li><li>Phytoextraction</li><li>Phytovolatilization</li><li>Phytodegradation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phytoremediation market encompasses various types of plant-based remediation techniques. Phytosequestration involves plants capturing and storing contaminants. Rhizodegradation utilizes root-associated microbes to degrade pollutants in the soil. Phytohydraulics focuses on using plants to control groundwater flow and stabilize contaminants. Phytoextraction enables plants to absorb and concentrate heavy metals from soil for later removal. Phytovolatilization involves the uptake of contaminants by plants, which release them as vapor. Phytodegradation entails direct degradation of pollutants by plant processes. Together, these methods offer eco-friendly solutions for environmental cleanup.</p></p>
