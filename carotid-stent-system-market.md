<p><ul><li>Straight Version</li><li>Tapered Version</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carotid stent system market features two main types: straight and tapered versions. The straight version is designed for uniform arterial sections, facilitating straightforward deployment in vessels with consistent diameters. In contrast, the tapered version accommodates arteries that have varying diameters, offering a more flexible fit and enhanced adaptability, particularly in areas with tapered anatomical features. Both types aim to improve blood flow and reduce the risk of vascular complications in patients with carotid artery stenosis.</p></p>
