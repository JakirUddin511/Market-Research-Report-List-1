<p><p>Additive Manufacturing Printers using metal powders encompass various technologies for producing complex metal parts. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) utilizes a high-powered laser to fuse metal powders layer by layer, allowing for intricate designs and strong mechanical properties. Electron Beam Melting (EBM), on the other hand, employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powders, offering faster build times and a high-quality finish. Other methods may include binder jetting and direct energy deposition, each providing unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
