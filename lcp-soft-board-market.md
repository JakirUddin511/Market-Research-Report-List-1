<p><p>LCP soft boards are categorized into three market types based on their layer configurations. Single-layer boards are composed of a single dielectric layer, providing basic functionality for low-frequency applications. Dual-layer boards feature two layers, enhancing performance and allowing for more complex circuitry. Multi-layer boards consist of multiple stacked layers, offering high-density interconnects, improved signal integrity, and the capability to support advanced applications. Each type serves different requirements, from simple designs to intricate, high-speed electronic systems.</p></p>
