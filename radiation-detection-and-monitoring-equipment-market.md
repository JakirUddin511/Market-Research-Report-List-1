<p><p>The radiation detection and monitoring equipment market includes various types of devices designed for safety and regulatory compliance. Personal dosimeters measure individual exposure to radiation, ensuring worker safety. Area process monitors assess radiation levels in specific locations, often in industrial settings. Environmental radiation monitors track ambient radiation in the environment. Surface contamination monitors detect radioactive contaminants on surfaces, while radioactive material monitors ensure safe handling and usage of radioactive substances. Together, these devices enhance safety and provide vital data for radiation management.</p></p>
