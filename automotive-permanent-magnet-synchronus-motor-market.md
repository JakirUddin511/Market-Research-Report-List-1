<p><strong>The Automotive Permanent Magnet Synchronus Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface PMSM (SPMSM) Type</li><li>Interior PMSM (IPMSM) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive permanent magnet synchronous motor (PMSM) market is primarily divided into two types: Surface PMSM (SPMSM) and Interior PMSM (IPMSM). SPMSMs feature magnets positioned on the rotor's surface, offering higher efficiency and simpler design but may have limitations in performance under high torque conditions. In contrast, IPMSMs integrate magnets within the rotor's interior, allowing for improved torque density and better performance at varied speeds. Both types cater to electric and hybrid vehicles, driving advancements in automotive technology.</p></p>
