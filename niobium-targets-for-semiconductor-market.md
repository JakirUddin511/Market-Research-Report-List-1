<p><p>Niobium targets are essential components in the semiconductor industry, used in physical vapor deposition processes. There are two main types: rotating targets and non-rotating targets. Rotating targets enhance uniformity and efficiency during deposition, allowing for consistent film quality and longer operational life. In contrast, non-rotating targets are simpler in design and more cost-effective, suitable for smaller production runs. Both types cater to various applications, including advanced electronic devices, by providing high-purity materials for sputtering and coating processes.</p></p>
