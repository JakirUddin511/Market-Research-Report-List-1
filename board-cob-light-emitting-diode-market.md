<p><p>The Board (COB) Light Emitting Diode market includes two primary types: Organic Light Emitting Diodes (OLEDs) and Inorganic Light Emitting Diodes (ILEDs). OLEDs are made from organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied, known for their flexibility, efficiency, and vibrant colors, making them suitable for displays and lighting. In contrast, ILEDs use inorganic materials, typically semiconductors, offering high brightness and durability, primarily used in industrial applications and outdoor signage. Together, they cater to diverse lighting and display needs.</p></p>
