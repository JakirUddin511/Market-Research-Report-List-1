<p><p>The non-woven filter fabrics market comprises various types, primarily needle punched nonwoven and spunbond nonwoven fabrics. Needle punched nonwoven fabrics are created by mechanically entangling fibers, offering excellent filtration efficiency and durability, making them suitable for applications like air and liquid filtration. Spunbond nonwoven fabrics are produced through the extrusion of polymer fibers, resulting in lightweight, strong materials. These fabrics are widely used in medical, automotive, and environmental applications due to their high tensile strength and versatility.</p></p>
