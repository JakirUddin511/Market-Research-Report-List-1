<p><p>Injection port markets primarily consist of two main types: I-Port Type and Insuflon Type. The I-Port Type allows for easy subcutaneous access, facilitating drug delivery without multiple needle sticks, making it ideal for chronic conditions. In contrast, the Insuflon Type is designed for insulin delivery, featuring a cannula that sits under the skin to provide continuous access for diabetic patients. Both types enhance patient comfort and adherence by minimizing the discomfort associated with frequent injections.</p></p>
