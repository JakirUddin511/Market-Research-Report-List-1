<p><p>The shuttleless loom market encompasses various technologies that enhance weaving efficiency. Rapier looms, available in single and double types, use a flexible rapier to carry the weft yarn across the warp. Air jet looms utilize high-speed air jets to propel the weft, offering quick production rates. Water jet looms employ a stream of water to insert the weft yarn, suitable for specific fabrics. Gripper or projectile looms use a gripper mechanism to transport the weft, combining speed and versatility in weaving applications.</p></p>
