Potash-mobilizing fertilizers focus on increasing potassium availability, which is essential for plant health and growth.</p><p>4. Other types may include various microbial inoculants and organic amendments that improve overall soil fertility and crop productivity. Together, these bio-fertilizers promote sustainable agriculture.</p></p>
