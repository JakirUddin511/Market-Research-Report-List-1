<p><p>The cat litter market comprises three primary types: clay cat litter, silica cat litter, and biodegradable cat litter. Clay cat litter, typically made from sodium bentonite, is highly absorbent and clumps, making it popular among cat owners. Silica cat litter contains tiny silica crystals that absorb moisture and odors while remaining dry. Biodegradable cat litter is made from natural materials such as paper, wood, or corn, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers seeking sustainable options for pet care.</p></p>
