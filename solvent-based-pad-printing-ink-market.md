<p><p>The solvent-based pad printing ink market is categorized into three main types: Single Component (1C), Two Component (2C), and 1-or 2-component (1-or 2-C). 1C inks consist of a single formulation that is ready to use, providing ease and efficiency. 2C inks require the mixing of a base and hardener, offering enhanced durability and adhesion. The 1-or 2-C type offers flexibility, allowing users to choose between single or dual components depending on specific printing requirements and desired properties.</p></p>
