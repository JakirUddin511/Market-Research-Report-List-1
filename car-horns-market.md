<p><p>The car horn market includes two main types: air horns and electric horns. Electric horns, on the other hand, rely on electromagnetic mechanisms to generate sound, offering a compact, reliable, and cost-effective solution for passenger cars and motorcycles. Both types cater to safety needs, with air horns excelling in volume and electric horns in versatility and ease of installation.</p></p>
