<p><p>The Automated Guided Vehicles (AGV) battery charger market is primarily divided into two types: contact chargers and non-contact chargers. Contact chargers require physical connections to charge the vehicle's batteries, offering efficient and reliable energy transfer. In contrast, non-contact chargers utilize technologies like inductive charging, allowing AGVs to recharge without direct contact, enhancing convenience and reducing wear. Both types cater to different operational needs, balancing efficiency, installation complexity, and maintenance requirements within various industrial applications.</p></p>
