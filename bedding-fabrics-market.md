<p><p>The bedding fabrics market encompasses two primary types: woven and knitted fabrics. Woven fabrics are crafted by interlacing threads at right angles, providing durability and a smooth finish, making them ideal for sheets and duvet covers. Knitted fabrics, on the other hand, are produced by looping yarn together, offering elasticity and softness, which enhances comfort in items like pillowcases and mattress covers. Both fabric types cater to diverse consumer preferences, influencing the style, feel, and functionality of bedding products.</p></p>
