Third-generation drugs, including lacosamide and perampanel, represent the latest advancements, often targeting specific seizure types with improved safety profiles. Each generation reflects progress in treatment options, focusing on effectiveness, minimizing side effects, and enhancing patient quality of life.</p></p>
