<p><ul><li>Self-Regulating Heaters</li><li>Constant Wattage Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric heat trace cable market features two main types: self-regulating heaters and constant wattage heaters. Self-regulating heaters automatically adjust their heat output depending on the surrounding temperature, ensuring efficient energy use and preventing overheating. In contrast, constant wattage heaters maintain a fixed output regardless of temperature changes, providing uniform heating but with a less adaptable performance. Both types are utilized in various industries for freeze protection, temperature maintenance, and process heating applications, ensuring equipment operates optimally in varying conditions.</p></p>
