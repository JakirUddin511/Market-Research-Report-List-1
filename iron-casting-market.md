<p><strong>The Iron Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gray iron</li><li>Ductile iron</li><li>Malleable iron</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The iron casting market is segmented into three main types: Gray iron, Ductile iron, and Malleable iron. Gray iron is known for its good machinability and high wear resistance, making it ideal for engine blocks and pipe fittings. Ductile iron, also called nodular cast iron, offers enhanced strength and flexibility, suitable for automotive and heavy machinery applications. Malleable iron, produced through heat treatment, provides excellent ductility and is commonly used for fittings and brackets in various industries. Each type serves unique applications based on their properties.</p></p>
