<p><p>The Infrastructure Mobile Substation market is primarily divided into two types: High Voltage (HV) Mobile Substations and Medium Voltage (MV) Mobile Substations. HV Mobile Substations are designed to handle voltages above 100 kV, providing temporary power solutions for high-demand applications such as during grid outages or maintenance. In contrast, MV Mobile Substations operate at voltages between 1 kV and 100 kV, serving urban and rural areas for local distribution needs, ensuring reliability and quick deployment in various scenarios.</p></p>
