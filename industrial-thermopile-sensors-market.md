<p><p>Industrial thermopile sensors are devices used to measure temperature by detecting infrared radiation emitted by objects. These sensors consist of multiple thermocouples connected in series, allowing them to convert thermal energy into measurable electrical signals. They are widely employed across various industries, including manufacturing, automotive, food processing, and HVAC systems, due to their robustness, high accuracy, and ability to operate without direct contact with the target surface.</p><p>The Industrial Thermopile Sensors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.60% during the forecast period.
