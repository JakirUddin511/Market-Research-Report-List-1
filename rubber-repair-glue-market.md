<p><p>Rubber repair glue is used to fix damaged rubber products, with two main market types: Hot Bond and Cold Bond. Hot Bond adhesives require heat activation, offering strong, durable repairs ideal for industrial applications like conveyor belts. Cold Bond adhesives cure at room temperature, providing quick, easy fixes for smaller repairs or delicate materials. Both types cater to different needs, balancing durability and convenience, making them essential for maintaining rubber-based equipment and products across various industries.</p></p>
