<p><strong>The Industrial White Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paraffin-based</li><li>Cycloalkyl</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial white oil market can be segmented into paraffin-based and cycloalkyl types. Paraffin-based white oils are derived from saturated hydrocarbons, known for their high purity, low volatility, and excellent stability, making them ideal for applications like cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. In contrast, cycloalkyl white oils, characterized by their ring-shaped hydrocarbon structures, offer unique thermal stability and reduced aromatic content. This makes them suitable for lubricants and industrial applications, where performance under diverse conditions is critical. Both types cater to distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
