<p><p>Vehicle undercarriage scanners come in three primary types based on their installation and mobility. Permanent scanners are embedded into the road surface, providing a seamless inspection process for vehicles passing over them. Fixed scanners, on the other hand, are attached to the road surface but can be relocated if necessary. Portable scanners are mobile devices that can be used in various locations, offering flexibility for temporary inspections. Each type serves distinct operational needs in security and maintenance applications.</p></p>
