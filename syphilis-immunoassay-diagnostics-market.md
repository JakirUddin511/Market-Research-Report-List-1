<p><p>The Syphilis Immunoassay Diagnostics market encompasses various testing techniques, primarily focusing on Nested Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) and Multiple PCR. The Nested PCR technique amplifies specific DNA sequences in a two-step process, enhancing sensitivity and specificity for detecting Treponema pallidum, the syphilis-causing pathogen. In contrast, Multiple PCR simultaneously detects multiple pathogens, facilitating the identification of co-infections. Both techniques offer advanced diagnostic capabilities, improving early detection rates and treatment outcomes in syphilis and associated sexually transmitted infections.</p></p>
