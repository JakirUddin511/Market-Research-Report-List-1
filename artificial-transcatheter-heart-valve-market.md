<p><p>The artificial transcatheter heart valve market is categorized into three main types: the aortic heart valve, mitral heart valve, and tricuspid heart valve. The aortic heart valve is primarily used to treat aortic stenosis, facilitating blood flow improvement. The mitral heart valve addresses mitral regurgitation and stenosis, enhancing heart function. The tricuspid heart valve, although less common, targets tricuspid regurgitation, ensuring effective blood circulation. Each type plays a crucial role in minimally invasive heart valve replacement therapies, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
