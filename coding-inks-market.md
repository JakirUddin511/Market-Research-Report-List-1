<p><p>Coding inks are essential for product marking and identification across various materials. The PVC type offers good adhesion to non-porous surfaces and is often used in plastic applications. XLPE type inks are tailored for cross-linked polyethylene, ensuring durability and resistance to harsh conditions. THHN type inks are designed for wiring and electrical applications, providing excellent heat and chemical resistance. Nylon rubber type inks are flexible and suited for rubber surfaces, allowing for high-quality prints that withstand wear and tear.</p></p>
