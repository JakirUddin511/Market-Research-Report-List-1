<p><p>The Food Grade Alkaline Protease market is categorized into various types based on their substrate specificity and function. Peptide enzymes target peptide bonds, facilitating protein breakdown into smaller peptides. The "Outside Peptidase" type acts on external bonds of proteins, while "Ester Bond of a Protein or Peptide" focuses on hydrolyzing ester linkages in lipid-bound proteins. Finally, the "Amide Bond of a Hydrolyzed Protein or Peptide" emphasizes the cleavage of amide bonds, enhancing protein digestibility for food applications.</p></p>
