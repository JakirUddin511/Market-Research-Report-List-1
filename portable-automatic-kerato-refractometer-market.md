<p><ul><li>Spherical Power</li><li>Cylindrical Power</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Automatic Kerato Refractometer market is primarily categorized into two types based on optical measurements: Spherical Power and Cylindrical Power. Spherical Power measures the overall curvature of the cornea, assessing vision correction needs for myopia or hyperopia. In contrast, Cylindrical Power evaluates asymmetry in the cornea to determine astigmatism. Both types enable precise vision assessment and are essential for personalized eyewear prescriptions, making them crucial tools in ophthalmology and optometry for enhancing patient care.</p></p>
