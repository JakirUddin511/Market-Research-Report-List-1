<p><p>The central venous catheter (CVC) market comprises several types based on the number of lumens: single-lumen, double-lumen, and triple-lumen catheters. Single-lumen catheters are used for basic access, suitable for delivering medication or fluids. Double-lumen catheters allow simultaneous administration of different therapies, enhancing patient management. Triple-lumen catheters offer additional versatility for multiple infusions and blood sampling. Other variations may include specialized designs for specific medical needs, expanding options for healthcare providers in diverse clinical settings.</p></p>
