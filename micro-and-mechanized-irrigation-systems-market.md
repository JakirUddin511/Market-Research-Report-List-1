<p><p>The micro and mechanized irrigation systems market encompasses various types designed for efficient water usage in agriculture. Sprinkler systems simulate rainfall, distributing water over crops through pipes and nozzles. Drip irrigation delivers water directly to plant roots via a network of tubes, minimizing evaporation and runoff. Central pivot systems rotate around a pivot point, irrigating large circular areas effectively. Lateral move systems consist of linear or lateral moving systems that spray water uniformly across fields, optimizing coverage and resource use.</p></p>
