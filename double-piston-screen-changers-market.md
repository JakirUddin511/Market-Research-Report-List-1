<p><p>Double piston screen changers are employed in various markets categorized as continuous and discontinuous. In continuous markets, these devices allow for uninterrupted material flow, enhancing production efficiency in processes like plastic extrusion and rubber manufacturing. Conversely, in discontinuous markets, screen changers operate in batch mode, pausing the flow during screen changes, which is typical in lower-volume applications. Both market types benefit from the ability to filter impurities and maintain product quality, though their operational dynamics differ significantly.</p></p>
