<p><ul><li>Caliper Integrated EPB</li><li>Cable Puller EPB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Parking Brake (EPB) market is primarily divided into two types: Caliper Integrated EPB and Cable Puller EPB. Caliper Integrated EPB combines the braking mechanism with the caliper, allowing for compact design and efficient operation, typically used in modern vehicles for space-saving benefits. In contrast, Cable Puller EPB utilizes traditional cable systems to engage the brakes electronically, offering a simpler installation and lower cost. Both types enhance vehicle safety and convenience, while responding to evolving automotive technology demands.</p></p>
