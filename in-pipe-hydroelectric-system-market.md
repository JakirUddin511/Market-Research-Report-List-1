<p><p>In-Pipe Hydroelectric Systems harness flowing water within pipelines to generate electricity. The market is divided into two main types: Internal Systems and External Systems. </p><p>Internal Systems are integrated directly within existing water pipelines, utilizing the existing flow for energy generation without significant infrastructure changes. External Systems, on the other hand, involve the installation of turbines outside the pipeline, often requiring additional infrastructure while still tapping into the hydraulic potential of the water flow. Both types aim to enhance renewable energy production and efficiency in water transportation.</p></p>
