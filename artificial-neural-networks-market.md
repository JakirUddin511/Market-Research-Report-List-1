<p><p>The artificial neural networks market can be categorized into three main types. Feed Forward Artificial Neural Networks process data in a unidirectional flow, making them suitable for tasks like classification and regression. Feedback Artificial Neural Networks, also known as recurrent networks, allow data to cycle back into the system, enhancing capabilities for sequence prediction and time-series analysis. Additionally, other market segments encompass specialized architectures like convolutional neural networks and generative adversarial networks, catering to specific applications in image processing and generative modeling.</p></p>
