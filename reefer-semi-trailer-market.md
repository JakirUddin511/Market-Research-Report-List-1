<p><p>The reefer semi-trailer market is divided into two main types: single temperature and multi-temperature. Single temperature reefer trailers maintain a consistent temperature throughout, ideal for transporting perishable goods that require uniform conditions, such as meat or dairy. In contrast, multi-temperature reefer trailers have separate compartments that can maintain different temperature zones simultaneously, allowing for the efficient transport of diverse products, like frozen goods alongside fresh produce. This versatility caters to the varied needs of the cold supply chain.</p></p>
