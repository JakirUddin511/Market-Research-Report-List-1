<p><p>The low voltage circuit breaker market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Molded-case Circuit Breakers (MCCB)** are designed for medium-voltage applications, providing protection against overload and short circuits, suitable for industrial settings. </p><p>2. **Air Circuit Breakers (ACB)** are used in high-current applications and operate by isolating faults in air, serving as a dependable choice for large-scale power distribution.</p><p>3. **Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCB)** protect against overcurrents in residential and commercial settings, efficiently handling minor faults and enhancing safety in electrical installations.</p></p>
