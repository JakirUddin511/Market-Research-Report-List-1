<p><p>A semi-balanced ship rudder is a type of rudder design that provides improved maneuverability and reduces the steering resistance experienced by a vessel. It typically features a pivot point located slightly ahead of the rudder’s center, allowing for a portion of the rudder surface to be submerged, thus enhancing performance during operation. This design minimizes the forces acting on the rudder shaft and can lead to lower operational costs and improved fuel efficiency.</p><p>The Semi-Balanced Ship Rudder Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period.
