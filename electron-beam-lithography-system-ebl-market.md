The market for EBL can be divided into two main types: Gaussian beam EBL systems, which utilize a focused, circular electron beam for uniform exposure, providing high precision but slower throughput; and shaped beam EBL systems, which employ tailored beams to optimize patterning speed and efficiency, making them suitable for larger-scale applications.</p></p>
