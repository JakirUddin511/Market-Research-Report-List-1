<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electronic Multiplier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM)</li><li>Continuous-dynode multipliers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic multiplier market is primarily divided into two types: Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM) and Continuous-Dynode Multipliers. CEMs use discrete channel structures to amplify charged particles, offering high gain and fast response times, making them ideal for applications like particle detection and mass spectrometry. Continuous-dynode multipliers, on the other hand, utilize a continuous surface for electron amplification, providing high sensitivity over a broader dynamic range. Both types are essential in scientific instrumentation and specialized detection systems.</p></p>
