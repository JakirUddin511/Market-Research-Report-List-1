<p><ul><li>Viscose Filament Yarn</li><li>Viscose Staple Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Viscose Fiber Market comprises two primary types: Viscose Filament Yarn (VFY) and Viscose Staple Fiber (VSF). VFY is a continuous, smooth yarn used in textiles like silk-like fabrics, linings, and apparel due to its lustrous finish and drapability. VSF, on the other hand, consists of short fibers blended with cotton or polyester for applications in clothing, home textiles, and non-woven products. Both are derived from wood pulp, offering eco-friendly, soft, and breathable alternatives to synthetic fibers, catering to diverse textile needs.</p></p>
