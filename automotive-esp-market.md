<p><p>The automotive Electronic Stability Program (ESP) market is categorized into 2-channel, 3-channel, and 4-channel systems based on the number of wheels controlled. A 2-channel ESP typically manages the rear wheels, ideal for lighter vehicles. A 3-channel system controls two rear wheels and one front wheel, enhancing stability in larger vehicles. The 4-channel system monitors all four wheels, providing comprehensive control and the highest level of safety, commonly found in modern passenger cars and SUVs for improved traction and handling.</p></p>
