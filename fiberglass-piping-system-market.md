<p><p>The fiberglass piping system market is primarily categorized into three types: GRE (Glass Reinforced Epoxy) pipes, GRP (Glass Reinforced Plastic) pipes, and others. GRE pipes are known for their high chemical resistance and mechanical strength, making them ideal for harsh environments. GRP pipes, on the other hand, are recognized for their lightweight, corrosion resistance, and durability, often used in water and wastewater applications. The "Others" category encompasses various specialty fiberglass piping products, tailored for specific industrial needs.</p></p>
