<p><ul><li>On-Premise</li><li>Cloud Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CRM software is categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise CRM requires installation on local servers, offering greater control and customization, but demands significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based CRM is hosted on the vendor's servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and lower initial costs. This model facilitates remote access and automatic updates, making it ideal for businesses seeking flexibility and collaboration without the burden of hardware management.</p></p>
