<p><ul><li>Cellular Communication Module</li><li>Non-cellular Communication Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT communication module market is divided into two main types: cellular and non-cellular modules. Cellular communication modules utilize mobile networks, ensuring wide coverage and reliable connectivity for IoT devices, making them suitable for applications requiring high mobility and real-time data transmission. In contrast, non-cellular communication modules rely on alternative methods such as Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or LoRa, focusing on short-range communication and lower power consumption, often used in smart home devices and localized applications.</p></p>
