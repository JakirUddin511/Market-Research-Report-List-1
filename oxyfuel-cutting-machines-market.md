CNC machines offer precision and automation, allowing for complex cuts with minimal operator intervention. Manually-controlled machines require direct operator input for cutting, providing flexibility but less efficiency. PLC-controlled systems combine automation and manual input, enabling complex operations with improved reliability. Each type caters to different industrial needs based on automation level and user expertise.</p></p>
