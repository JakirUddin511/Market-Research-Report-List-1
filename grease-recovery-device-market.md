<p><p>The grease recovery device market can be categorized into two primary types: installed underground and freestanding systems. Installed underground devices are designed for sub-surface installation, providing discreet and efficient grease management, often integrated into the waste plumbing system. In contrast, freestanding systems are surface-mounted, offering flexibility in placement and ease of access for maintenance. Both types serve the same purpose of capturing and separating grease from wastewater, thereby preventing clogs and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations in commercial kitchens and food processing facilities.</p></p>
