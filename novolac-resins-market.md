<p><strong>The Novolac Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic</li><li>Thermosetting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Novolac resins are categorized into two primary types: thermoplastic and thermosetting. Thermoplastic novolac resins remain pliable upon heating, allowing for reprocessing and recycling, making them suitable for applications requiring flexibility and resistance. In contrast, thermosetting novolac resins undergo a curing process that hardens them permanently, providing superior durability and heat resistance. Each type serves distinct markets, with thermoplastics primarily used in consumer goods and coatings, while thermosettings are favored in automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications due to their structural integrity.</p></p>
