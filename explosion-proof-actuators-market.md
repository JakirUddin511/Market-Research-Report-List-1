Linear actuators, on the other hand, create linear motion, facilitating tasks like lifting or pushing in hazardous environments. Both types are designed to operate safely in explosive atmospheres, ensuring reliability and compliance with safety standards, making them essential in industries like oil and gas, chemicals, and mining.</p></p>
