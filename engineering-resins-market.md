<p><p>The engineering resins market includes various types of polymers with distinct properties. Polyethylene (PE) is categorized into LDPE (flexible, low density), MDPE (moderate strength), HDPE (high strength, rigid), LLDPE (tough, flexible), and UHMWPE (ultra-high molecular weight, wear-resistant). Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is versatile, used in rigid and flexible forms for construction and piping. Polybutylene Terephthalate (PBT) is a thermoplastic with excellent electrical and mechanical properties, ideal for automotive and electronics. Each resin type serves specific industrial applications based on durability, flexibility, and chemical resistance.</p></p>
