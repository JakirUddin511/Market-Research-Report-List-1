<p><p>The inorganic photosensitive drum market comprises various types, each with unique properties and applications. Selenium drums offer excellent light sensitivity and stability, making them common in photocopiers. Selenium alloys enhance performance under varying conditions. Cadmium sulfide drums provide good performance but face environmental concerns due to cadmium toxicity. Amorphous silicon drums are favored for their flexibility and durability, suitable for high-volume printing. Each type caters to specific requirements in the printing and imaging industries.</p></p>
