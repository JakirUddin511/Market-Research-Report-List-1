<p><ul><li>Fluorocarbon Elastomers</li><li>Fluorosilicone Elastomers</li><li>Perfluoroelastomers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluoroelastomer market comprises three main types: fluorocarbon elastomers, fluorosilicone elastomers, and perfluoroelastomers. Fluorocarbon elastomers are known for their excellent chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making them suitable for various industrial applications. Fluorosilicone elastomers offer added flexibility and resistance to fuel and oils, suitable for automotive and aerospace applications. Perfluoroelastomers provide superior performance in extreme environments, with exceptional chemical and thermal stability, making them ideal for specialized industries like oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
