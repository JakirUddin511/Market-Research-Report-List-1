<p><p>SaaS Enterprise applications are categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions involve software installed locally on a company’s servers, offering greater control and customization at the cost of higher maintenance responsibilities. In contrast, cloud-based applications are hosted on remote servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and reduced upfront costs, while the vendor manages updates and security. The choice between these types depends on organizational needs, resources, and preference for control versus convenience.</p></p>
