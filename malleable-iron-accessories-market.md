<p><p>The malleable iron accessories market is categorized primarily into three types: White Heart Malleable Iron, Black Heart Malleable Iron, and Pearlitic Malleable Iron. White Heart Malleable Iron features a microstructure that enhances ductility and is used in applications requiring high strength. Black Heart Malleable Iron, produced through a longer annealing process, exhibits improved toughness, making it suitable for heavy-duty components. Pearlitic Malleable Iron, characterized by its combination of strength and hardness, is ideal for applications demanding durability and wear resistance.</p></p>
