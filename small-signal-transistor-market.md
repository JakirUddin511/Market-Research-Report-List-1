<p><ul><li>Bipolar Transistor</li><li>Unipolar Transistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small signal transistor market is primarily categorized into bipolar and unipolar transistors. Bipolar transistors, including NPN and PNP types, utilize both electron and hole charge carriers, making them suitable for analog applications with significant current control. Unipolar transistors, such as MOSFETs and JFETs, operate using a single type of charge carrier, offering high input impedance and efficient switching capabilities, ideal for digital circuits. Together, these types cater to diverse applications, from amplification to signal processing.</p></p>
