<p><strong>The Refrigerator Water Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) Filters</li><li>Carbon Block Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigerator water filters market primarily features two types: Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) filters and Carbon Block filters. GAC filters use loose granules of activated carbon to adsorb impurities, effectively reducing chlorine, sediment, and volatile organic compounds. In contrast, Carbon Block filters leverage a solid block of activated carbon to provide a finer filtration process, enhancing the removal of contaminants and improving taste and odor. Both types are essential for ensuring safe, clean drinking water from refrigerators.</p></p>
