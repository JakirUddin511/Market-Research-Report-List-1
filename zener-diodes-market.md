<p><p>The Zener Diodes market is primarily categorized into two types: Through Hole Technology (THT) and Surface Mount Technology (SMT). THT components are designed for insertion into drilled holes on printed circuit boards, offering robust connections and ease of handling, often preferred in high-power applications. Conversely, SMT diodes are soldered directly onto the surface of PCBs, allowing for smaller, lighter, and more compact designs, ideal for modern electronics. Each technology serves distinct applications based on size, performance, and manufacturing preferences.</p></p>
