In contrast, high capacitance MLCCs, exceeding 1μF, are utilized in power supply circuits and energy storage, supporting advanced features in electric vehicles. Both segments are critical for enhancing performance and reliability in modern automotive electronics, meeting evolving industry demands.</p></p>
