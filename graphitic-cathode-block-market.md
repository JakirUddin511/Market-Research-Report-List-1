<p><ul><li>Bottom Block</li><li>Side Block</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Graphitic Cathode Block market is primarily segmented into two types: Bottom Blocks and Side Blocks. Bottom Blocks are designed to be installed at the base of electrolytic cells, providing essential conductivity and structural support. They are typically thicker and larger, enhancing thermal management. Side Blocks, on the other hand, are installed vertically along the sides of the cells, contributing to overall electrical efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in the performance of aluminum electrolysis processes, influencing productivity and energy consumption.</p></p>
