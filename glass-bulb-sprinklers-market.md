<p><p>Glass bulb sprinklers are crucial components of fire protection systems, categorized into four main types. Pendent sprinklers hang from the ceiling, discharging water downward. Upright sprinklers are mounted above ceiling level and spray water upwards. Horizontal sidewall sprinklers are installed on walls, providing coverage for specific areas. Concealed sprinklers are hidden within the ceiling structure, only activating when necessary for aesthetic purposes. Each type is designed for particular applications, ensuring effective fire suppression in various environments.</p></p>
