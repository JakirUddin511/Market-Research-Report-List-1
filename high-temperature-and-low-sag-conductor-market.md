<p><p>The high-temperature and low-sag conductor market includes various types designed to perform efficiently under elevated temperatures while minimizing sag. ACSS (Aluminum Conductor Steel Supported) enhances strength without compromising conductivity. ACSS/TW (Twisted Wire) improves thermal performance. GTACSR (Geometrically Tapered Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced) optimizes conductor geometry for better efficiency. ACCC (Aluminum Conductor Composite Core) integrates composite materials for lightweight and durability. GZTACSR (Galvanized Steel Tapered Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced) offers corrosion resistance and strength, alongside other specialized varieties tailored for specific transmission needs.</p></p>
