<p><p>The sawmill blade market primarily consists of circular saw blades and band saw blades, each serving distinct purposes in wood processing. Circular saw blades are characterized by their round shape, suitable for making straight cuts in various materials, offering high efficiency and precision. In contrast, band saw blades consist of a continuous loop of metal, ideal for intricate cuts and curves, allowing for versatility in shaping wood. Both types cater to different industrial needs, enhancing productivity and accuracy in sawmilling operations.</p></p>
