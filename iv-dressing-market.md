<p><p>The I.V. dressing market can be categorized into two primary types: transparent and translucent dressings. Transparent dressings are clear and allow for direct visibility of the wound site, facilitating monitoring without the need for removal. They are moisture-resistant and comfortable for patients. Translucent dressings, on the other hand, offer partial visibility and provide a balance between protection and observation. Both types are designed to maintain a sterile environment, protect against external contaminants, and support the healing process while ensuring patient comfort.</p></p>
