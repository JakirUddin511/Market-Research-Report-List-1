<p><p>The automobile engine camshaft market includes three main types: Cast Camshafts, Forged Steel Camshafts, and Assembled Camshafts. Cast camshafts are made by pouring molten metal into molds, offering cost-effectiveness and suitability for standard engines. Forged steel camshafts are created by shaping heated steel under high pressure, providing superior strength and durability for high-performance engines. Assembled camshafts are built by combining separate components, allowing customization and flexibility for specialized applications. Each type caters to different engine requirements, balancing performance, cost, and manufacturing complexity.</p></p>
