<p><strong>The Automotive Air Conditioning System (A & C) Parts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Orifice Tube System Type A/C</li><li>Expansion Valve System Type A/C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air conditioning parts market is primarily divided into two types: Orifice Tube System Type A/C and Expansion Valve System Type A/C. The Orifice Tube system utilizes a simple tube to regulate refrigerant flow, providing cost-effective cooling, ideal for compact vehicles. In contrast, the Expansion Valve system employs a thermostatic valve to adjust refrigerant flow more precisely, allowing for better temperature control and efficiency, making it suitable for higher-end vehicles. Each type addresses different performance and budget needs in the automotive sector.</p></p>
