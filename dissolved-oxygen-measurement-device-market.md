<p><strong>The Dissolved Oxygen Measurement Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polographic</li><li>Galvanic</li><li>Optical(Luminescent)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dissolved oxygen measurement device market comprises three main types: polarographic, galvanic, and optical (luminescent). Polarographic sensors utilize an electrochemical method that requires a sample to be stirred for accurate measurement. Galvanic sensors operate similarly but do not require stirring, using a chemical reaction for readings. Optical sensors employ luminescence to detect dissolved oxygen levels without the need for calibration against temperature and pressure changes, offering greater accuracy and longer operational lifetimes, making them increasingly popular in various applications.</p></p>
