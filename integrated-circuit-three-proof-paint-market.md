<p><strong>The Integrated Circuit Three Proof Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Three Proof Paint</li><li>UV Curing Three Proof Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated Circuit (IC) three proof paint market comprises two main types: thermosetting three proof paint and UV curing three proof paint. Thermosetting three proof paint offers excellent durability and resistance to environmental factors through a chemical curing process when heat is applied. In contrast, UV curing three proof paint utilizes ultraviolet light to rapidly cure the paint, providing quick processing times and strong adhesion. Both types are essential for protecting ICs from moisture, dust, and chemicals in various electronic applications.</p></p>
