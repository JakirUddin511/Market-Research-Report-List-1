<p><p>The Chip-on-Board (CoB) LED market is categorized into three main types: Lateral Chip, Vertical Chip, and Flip Chip. </p><p>Lateral Chips have a horizontal orientation, ideal for applications requiring compact designs. Vertical Chips provide better thermal management and light extraction, making them suitable for high-power applications. Flip Chips feature a unique design where the chip is flipped, allowing for efficient heat dissipation and higher lumen output. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to diverse lighting requirements across various industries.</p></p>
